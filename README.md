# 🚗 Uber Smart Pricing & Demand System

## 🌐 Live App
👉 [https://uber-smart-pricing-system-9qrjyy8tnjftt97yabbvl8.streamlit.app/]

## 📌 Business Problem
Uber loses revenue daily by charging
the same price whether 10 or 500 people
need a ride - leaving serious money
on the table.

## 🎯 Key Findings
- Non-peak fare ($11.63) > Peak fare ($11.05)
  → $98,000 revenue opportunity identified!
- 5AM rides = highest avg fare ($15.78)
- 3 customer personas discovered
- Friday = highest revenue day
- Premium trips = 9x revenue per ride
